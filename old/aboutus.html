About Oculus.

Company Profile


Oculus PharmaCare Limited was established in the year 2000.
The principal activity of the company is marketing and distribution of Pharmaceuticals and Allied products
Fully owned by Nigerians




Who are we?

How were we formed?

NOMEDI Pharmaceuticals Ltd is a subsidiary of Oculus PharmaCare Ltd.
It was incorporated in February 2010 (RC 871751) and started operations the same year.
Business Activities – Nomedi is primarily engaged in the importation of pharmaceuticals, and in the distribution of same in Nigeria.
In recent times, however, the company has also diversified into the importation and distribution
of Medical Devices, Herbal products and Neutraceuticals.

Suppliers of Nomedi Products;

  • Saga Laboratories Ltd, India

  • MBL Pharma, Pakistan

  • ATCO Laboratories Ltd. Pakistan

  • Novins International Pvt Ltd, Pakistan

Management

1. Mr C.C. Nzimako (Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer):

Mr Nzimako holds a Bachelor of science degree in Business Administration and MBA in Finance. He has over thirty years cognate experience. His working career started in Lever Brothers Nigeria Plc(Unilever) as a Management Trainee and before resigning he held various appointments in Materials Management and Distribution. He joined Swiss Nigerian Chemical co Ltd.(CIBA) in 1983 and held various management positions in Distribution, Operations and prior to the merger of CIBA and Sandoz to form Novartis he was Acting Head of Pharma Division of CIBA.



2. Mr. V.I. Ezeiru FFPCPharm, FPSN:(Sales/Regulatory Affairs Director):

Mr Ezeiru brings to the management of the organization over 30 years experience gained in Sales and Regulatory Affairs. He joined CIBA in 1983 as a medical representative and was at various times Area Manager Lagos/West Zone and Sales Manager. He has a B.Pharm Degree and is a Fellow of West African Postgraduate College Pharmacists. He is also a Fellow of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria.
