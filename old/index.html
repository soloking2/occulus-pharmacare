Companies Represented in Nigeria :


Acino Pharma AG

A Swiss based pharmaceutical company that develops, manufactures and internationally markets well-proven and innovative pharmaceuticals in novel drug delivery forms.
  • Acino is a leader in advanced drug delivery technologies with a focus on modified release oral forms, oral dispersible forms and extended release parenterals for which it holds patents.
  • Acino markets Swiss-quality medicines in up and coming economies, currently 80 countries in the middle East, Africa, Latin America and Asia
  • The Acino group has 788 employees and generated revenues of EUR 143 million in the first half of the year 2013.

Pharma Bavaria International Group

A Marketing Authorization Holder, that distributes and promotes high-quality pharmaceuticals with well researched ingredients in more than 45 countries.
  • Pharma Bavaria International provides strong pharmaceutical premium brands based on established and well researched ingredients.
  • Pharma Bavaria focuses on growth regions such as the Middle East,Africa,South East Asia,CIS countries and other emerging economies
  • With a vision of "Access to Quality Healthcare", Pharma Bavaria offers products at fair prices.
