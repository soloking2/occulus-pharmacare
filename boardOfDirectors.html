Board Of Directors

Abdul Latiff A. Muse

Chairman

Alhaji Muse is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria with MSc. Degree in Finance and Banking. He has over 30 years experience in Banking and was at one time Treasurer at NAL Merchant Bank plc. and Managing Director Access Bank Plc. He is Chairman Pillars Finance and Investor Services Ltd. and Real LAAM & Associates. He is also presently involved in Community Services in Victoria Island and Ikoyi, Lagos.

Mohammed Y. Budah

Vice Chairman

Alhaji Budah is a Pharmacist and prior to going into private business worked for many years with CIBA as a Medical Reprsentative He is the Chief Executive of Niyya Food and Drinks Coy. Ltd., Kaduna and Osbud-K Pharmacy Ltd. Kaduna. He is a Fellow of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria and was at one time the National President of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria. He is a member of the National Institute(Mni).

Emma K.O Ivi

Director

Mazi Ivi holds a B.Sc. and MBA and is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria. He was for eight years a Senior Accountant with Texaco Overseas in charge of cash calls and Joint Vnture Operations and for eleven years Corporate Treaurer at CIBA. He is Chairman/Chief Executive Officer Target international Ltd. and its Group of Companies. He is a consultant and Board Member of Abbey Mortgage Bank Plc.

Clifford C. Nzimako

Former MD/CEO

Mr Nzimako holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration and MBA in Finance. He has over thirty years cognate experience. His working career started in Lever Brothers Nigeria Plc (Unilever) as a Management Trainee and held various appointments in Materials Management and Distribution. He joined Swiss Coy Ltd. (CIBA) in 1983 and held various management positions in Distribution, Operations and prior to the merger of CIBA and Sandoz to form Novartis he was Acting Head of Pharma Division of CIBA.

Okanlawon Y. Oni

Marketing Director

Alhaji Oni has more than 30 years working expereince starting with Swiss Nigerian ChemicalCo. Ltd. where he rose from CibaVision Representative in 1982 to Head of CIBAVision Unit in Pharma Division. He has been instrumental in the development of CibaVision busness in Nigeria. He has an MSc. Degree in Bacteriology from Teas Southern University, Houston, Texas.

Augustine Nzobiwu

Director

Mr. Nzobiwu is a Pharmacist of many years standing and Managing Director of PharmaVision Ltd., Lagos. Prior to this, Mr. Nzobiwu worked for many years at CIBA where he was at various times Head Servipharm Business Unit and National Sales Manager.

Godwin A.N. Osuji FPSN

Director

Mr. Osuji is a Pharmacist who worked for many years with Glaxo Nigeria Ltd. as Medical Rep. (North), Area Sales Manager (West), Branch Manager (East), Regional Manager (West) and Export Sales Manager. He is currently Managing Director of Osbud Pharmacy Ltd., Lagos. He is a Fellow of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria.

We were established in the year 2000 with the aim of marketing and distributing pharmaceuticals and allied products. We are a nigerian owned business with international affiliations.
